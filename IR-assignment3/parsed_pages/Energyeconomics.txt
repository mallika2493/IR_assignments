history
energy related issues have been actively present in economic literature since the 1973 oil crisis but have their roots much further back in the history as early as 1865 ws jevons expressed his concern about the eventual depletion of coal resources in his book the coal question one of the best known early attempts to work on the economics of exhaustible resources  incl fossil fuel  was made by h hotelling who derived a price path for non-renewable resources known as hotelling s rule
